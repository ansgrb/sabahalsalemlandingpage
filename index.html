A Redesign Proposal for Sabah Al Salem

A comprehensive design case study and interactive prototype aimed at enhancing the user experience for both customers and stakeholders.

The Vision

This project is a dedicated effort to reimagine the Sabah Al Salem digital experience. Based on a deep case study of customer needs and business objectives, this design introduces two distinct, user-centric interfaces: a streamlined journey for customers and a powerful dashboard for stakeholders.

Two Experiences, One Platform

The redesign addresses the unique needs of each user group within a single, cohesive application.

For the Customer

An intuitive and seamless shopping experience designed for ease of use and engagement.

For the Stakeholder

A powerful, data-driven dashboard for managing operations and monitoring performance.
